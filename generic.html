Turn Your furs into cash Today!

Cash For Fur Coats has been Recycling & Upcycling pre-owned and new fur coats since 1999! Recycling & Upcycling makes complete sense, someone else can have the pleasure of doing something different and creative with that old fur coat, which is just taking up space in your closet gathering dust and drying out. We pay cash & reimburse any shipping cost for any fur coat or coats that are sent in to our warehouse to buy at Cash For Fur Coats..

How it Works:

Please submit your information below along with a detailed description of the furs in your posession. Please be prepared to provide photographs in the follow up e-mail to verify condition! If your fur matches our criteria we will request you ship the coat to our warehouse. Once the coat has been received it will be thoroughly inspected and if satisfied an offer will be made. Once accepted payment will be made via your paypal account.
